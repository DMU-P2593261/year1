It's the year 2045 and the world is at a point of utter chaos, but the population find their escape in the OASIS: a virtual reality universe built by the eccentric creator James Haliday.

When Haliday passes away, he vouches to give his immense fortune to the first individual to discover an easter egg that's hidden deep within the OASIS's many realms.

When Wade Watts enters the competition, he discovers himself becoming the hero in a reality-bending treasure hunt through a land of friendship, mystery and danger.
