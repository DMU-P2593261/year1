Tropicana

Tropciana is the name of my Animal Crossing: New Horizons island. This website was created based off of my enjoyment of the game.

On this page you can find a breakdown of my Animal Crossing journey and what my island is all about.

Island Entrance

On Tropicana, many different buildings and decorations are on display and every corner is filled with trees, flowers and paths. The entrance to Tropicana is one of the most spectacular parts of the island. It features a duel level waterfall eitherside which has been decorated with nova lights, shrubs and flowers.

Buildings and Decorations

All around the island there are many different buildings. The most notible ones such as the plaza, shop and museum have a huge part in the economy of the game and play a huge part in allowing players to progress.

Resident Services

The airport is one of the fundemental building blocks of every island. The airport is where players will first start when opening up the game for the first time. Once the tutorial for the game has ended the airport opens up fully and allows players to travel back and forth to islands owned by other players. That is assuming they have a Nintendo Online subscription.

The Airport

All around the island there are many different buildings. The most notible ones such as the plaza, shop and museum have a huge part in the economy of the game and play a huge part in allowing players to progress.

Museum

The museum is a building owned by Blathers the owl. Blathers collects fossils, bugs, paintings and fish. All items of these categories if donated to him are displayed within the museum. Blathers will also check fossils for you so you can sell them at the store later on.

Nook's Cranny

Nook's Cranny is the Animal Crossing equivalent of a supermarket. All resources such as furniture, tools, wallpaper and scenery such as flowers and bushes can be purchased here. Selling is also a large part of this building, the nooklings will buy almost any item from you for a set price.

Able Sisters

The Able Sisters is a tailors shop owned by a couple of female hedgehogs. Between them they run the shop and offer you a variety of services. Within the shop players can buy clothes and browse community pattern designs at the custom designs kiosk.

The Campsite

The campsite is what you'd expect from it. With no random interval random villagers will appear in the capsite. Players will then have the option of talking to them and inviting them to stay on their island.

Daily Island Routine

Playing on Animal Crossing: New Horizons provides players with a way of escaping their day to day lives and living a virtual life with no restrictions. This is especially useful at the moment given the current conditions of the pandemic.

In order to make the most of the game and to keep your island in good shape, logging in daily is recommended by a lot of players. I myself as an Animal Crossing player, login daily to complete my set of daily tasks.

Firstly I login and speak to all my favourite villagers to make sure their friend status is kept high and then I collect my daily login bonus from the resident services. If I have any spare time I will also pick up and weeds on my island, collect fruit from the trees and sell any resourses found to make some exrta money.
